## ğŸ“ Description

Prayas-Sankalp is a compassionate platform built with Express.js, designed to bridge the gap between donors and those in need. We facilitate the donation of essential resources such as food, clothing, stationery, and other vital supplies to uplift underprivileged communities. Our mission is to cultivate a culture of care and empathy by providing a seamless and reliable channel for donors to directly contribute to the well-being of individuals and families facing hardship. Join us in making a tangible difference and fostering a more equitable society through Prayas-Sankalp.
